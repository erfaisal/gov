About the Ministry

The Ministry of Identity & Citizenship is the foundation of Cupx Nation. We provide every citizen with a unique, secure, and verifiable digital identity (idex) and maintain the national DigiLocker for all important documents.

Your idex is linked to your citizens table, bank account, amazx purchases, ridex rides, and every other service in the Cupx ecosystem.

Core Functions

  • → Issuance of idex (Universal Citizen ID)
  • → DigiLocker – Secure document vault
  • → Verification & KYC services for all ministries
  • → Birth, Death, Marriage & other certificates
  • → Single sign-on across entire Cupx ecosystem
