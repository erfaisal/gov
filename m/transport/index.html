About the Ministry

The Ministry of Transport & Infrastructure is responsible for all modes of transportation and digital connectivity within Cupx Nation. We issue driving permits, commercial vehicle licenses, railway and airline ticketing regulations, and manage broadband/ISP services.

Every ride, delivery, journey, and internet connection in the Cupx ecosystem is governed and verified through this ministry.

Core Responsibilities

  • → Issuance of driving licenses and vehicle permits
  • → Regulation of railway and airline services
  • → Management of ISP and broadband infrastructure
  • → Commercial transport operator approvals
  • → Road safety and transport safety standards
