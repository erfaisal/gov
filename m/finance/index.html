About the Ministry

The Ministry of Finance & Economy is responsible for the economic governance of Cupx Nation. We operate the Central Bank, regulate all financial institutions, collect taxes, and provide financial services to citizens and businesses.

Our vision is to create a fully integrated, secure, and transparent financial ecosystem where every transaction flows seamlessly between bank.cupx.in, cpays, amazx, and other services.

Key Responsibilities

  • → Collection and management of taxes (taxx)
  • → Issuance of business and seller licenses
  • → Distribution of subsidies and welfare
  • → Oversight of your Central Bank
