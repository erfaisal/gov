About the Ministry

The Ministry of Justice & Grievance upholds the rule of law in Cupx Nation. We provide digital courts for dispute resolution, a grievance redressal system, and protect citizen rights across banking, e-commerce, transport, and all other services.

Every complaint against a seller on amazx, a driver on ridex, or any service in the ecosystem is handled transparently through this ministry.
