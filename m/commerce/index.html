About the Ministry

The Ministry of Commerce & Industry drives economic activity in Cupx Nation. We provide the regulatory framework for e-commerce, manufacturing, and trade so that businesses can thrive while maintaining quality and trust for citizens.

Every seller on amazx, every manufacturer supplying products, and every business operating in the Cupx ecosystem must be registered and approved through this ministry.

Core Responsibilities

  • → Seller registration and verification for amazx
  • → Manufacturer licensing and approvals
  • → Issuance of quality seals and certifications
  • → Business license management
  • → Import/export and trade facilitation
